Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?
Dressy / festive attire is perfect. Think dressy synagogue for the service, then comfortable enough for dancing and celebrating.
What is the schedule?
Please arrive 5:30 PM sharp for the service, followed by cocktail hour and games, then dinner, speeches, and dancing.
Where do I book a hotel?
We recommend Cherry Hill / Mount Laurel / nearby Philly suburbs.
How does pickup work?
Pickup is easiest at the main entrance of Congregation Kol Ami.
